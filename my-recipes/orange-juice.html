ORANGE JUICE

Fresh orange juice takes a little work, but it tastes much better than orange juice from concentrate.
I came up with this recipe in response to a request about how to make fresh-squeezed orange juice.
You may also use a citrus reamer to do this. If you want less pulp, use a hand juicer with a strainer.

Ingredients

  • 4 oranges
  • Sugar(optional)

Steps To Make Fresh Orange Juice

  1. Lightly smack each orange on the counter.
  2. Cut each orange in half and squeeze juice into a glass.
  3. Add Sugar to taste(optional).
